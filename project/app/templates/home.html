ABOUT US

A legacy of more than 38 years, The Sparkling journey of Kalamandir Jewellers started in 1986 in the small town of Kosamba near Surat, Gujarat. A team of 5 members with solid willpower, knowledge, and vision started the journey to becoming India's leading jewelry retail brand with a small store of around 200 Sq. ft. Kalamandir Jewellers leaped the growth by building years of Trust, Reliability, and Quality among the minds of customers; Currently, the Brand has more than 1200+ dedicated staff to serve the customers. The company has introduced some of the most prominent National jewelry brands like Rishtadiamonds, Kingly, Indo-Italia, Purusham, Platinum & Sajdhajke with this and the constant love of customers, Kalamandir jewellers is today GUJARAT'S leading Retail Jewellery Brand
